Marine Electrical

Boat & Marine Electrician Services in Miami

Marine electrical problems can range from a nuisance (flickering nav lights) to a serious safety hazard (bilge pump failure, engine-start issues). Our licensed marine electricians troubleshoot, repair, and upgrade all onboard electrical systems — ABYC-standard wiring, battery banks, shore power, and more.

We also handle full electronics installations: chartplotters, multifunction displays, VHF radios, radar, AIS, NMEA 2000 networks, fish finders, autopilots, and LED lighting upgrades. Neat, waterproof, label-bundled work — done right the first time.
