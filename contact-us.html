Common Questions

FAQs

Do you come to me, or do I have to bring my boat somewhere? +
We come to you. Our mobile marine mechanics travel to your dock, marina, slip, storage yard, or even a boat ramp. We service all of Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties.
What are your service hours? +
We operate Monday–Sunday, 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM. Emergency boat repair is available 24/7 — call (305) 290-2701 any time.
How quickly can you respond? +
For routine service we typically schedule within 1–2 business days. For emergencies — engine failure before a fishing trip, offshore breakdowns — we aim to respond same-day. Call us and explain the urgency.
What brands do you service? +
We're certified on Yamaha, Mercury, Suzuki, and Honda 4-stroke outboards (2.5–600hp), plus MerCruiser, Volvo Penta, Yanmar, Cummins, Crusader, and PCM inboard engines.
Do you provide estimates before starting work? +
Always. We perform a diagnostic inspection and provide a transparent quote before any parts are ordered or work begins. No surprise invoices.
